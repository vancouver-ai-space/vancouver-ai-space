Overview

Vancouver AI Space 001 is an intensive 6-week online program designed to accelerate your AI learning journey. While demanding, this program guarantees significant growth in your AI capabilities and project-building skills for those willing to put in the work.

This program offers:

  • Intensive hands-on experience building real AI projects from ideation to implementation
  • Direct mentorship from research and industry experts who are active in the field
  • Structured learning path with weekly lectures and hands-on labs
  • Flexible online environment where you can work at your own pace solo or with a team
  • Networking opportunities with other passionate AI builders and practitioners

What to Expect:

  • Fast-paced, project-focused learning environment
  • Assignments that build practical AI development skills
  • Regular feedback and guidance from experienced mentors
  • Strong emphasis on learning by doing
  • Supportive community of fellow learners

Program Benefits:

  • No in-person attendance required
  • Free of charge (Your Commitment is what is Needed)
  • Community-driven learning experience
  • Practical, portfolio-ready projects
  • Lasting connections with the AI community

Note: While this program is intensive and requires dedication, participants who fully engage with the material and complete the assignments can expect significant improvement in their AI development capabilities.

Certificate of Completion

To earn your Vancouver AI Space 001 Certificate of Completion, you must achieve a minimum overall grade of 70%. The grading scheme is structured as follows:

Weekly Grading Components:

  • Main Assignment (60% each week)
    • Project-based assignments
    • Late submissions receive a 40% grade instead of 60%
    • Due every Saturday
  • Peer Feedback (40% each week)
    • Provide constructive feedback on other participants' work
    • Due every Sunday
  • Bonus Assignment (25% extra each week)
    • Optional additional challenges
    • Opportunity to boost your weekly grade

Special Recognition:

  • The top 3 performing participants will receive special awards
  • Top performers will be featured on our website and social media channels
  • Recognition certificates will highlight exceptional achievement

Note: A minimum grade of 70% is required to pass the course and receive the certificate of completion.
